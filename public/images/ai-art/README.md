# AI Art Showcase Images

This folder contains the AI-generated art showcase images for the AI Art section carousel.

## Image Files Required

Place your 5 GQ magazine-style AI art images in this folder:

1. `ai-art-1.jpg` - The New Modern Man (Red theme)
2. `ai-art-2.jpg` - The New Face of Style (Blue theme)
3. `ai-art-3.jpg` - Modern Gentleman (Black/White theme)
4. `ai-art-4.jpg` - The Velvet Revolution (Green theme)
5. `ai-art-5.jpg` - Style Secrets for Men (Green theme)

## How to Add Images

Simply copy your AI-generated GQ magazine cover images to this folder with the names above.
The carousel will automatically load and display them in a rotating slideshow.

## Image Specifications

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 500x700px or similar (portrait orientation)
- All images should be the same aspect ratio for best carousel display
